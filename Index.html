For the past several years, I’ve been given the grace to create art, music, and poetry as a means of healing and building community. Inspired by 9th Wonder, Big K.R.I.T., Erick the Architect, J Dilla, and many others.

The goal is simple: to heal, to connect, to create, and to honor the culture that made space for this work to exist. Join me, and let’s build something magical through sound.
